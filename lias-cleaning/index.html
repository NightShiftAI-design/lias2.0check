How We Determine
Your Cleaning Level

Every home is different — our pricing reflects that honestly. We ask a few questions before booking to quote you fairly.

📋 What We May Ask Before Booking
📐 Size of home (sq ft)
🛏️ Bedrooms & bathrooms
📅 Last time professionally cleaned
🐾 Pets in the home
📸 Photos of key areas
⚠️ Areas of special concern
Level 01

Light Cleaning

Home is generally maintained with minimal buildup. Recently kept up with regular tidying.

  • Standard rates apply
  • Quicker turnaround
  • Great for recurring clients
Standard Rates
Level 02 — Most Common

Moderate Buildup

Some grease, soap scum, or areas not cleaned in a while. Requires more time and product.

  • Deep clean recommended
  • Adjusted quote provided
  • May require extra time
Adjusted Pricing
Level 03

Heavy / Deep Clean

Significant buildup or never professionally cleaned. Requires extensive labor and specialized products.

  • Specialized deep clean
  • Custom quote after photos
  • May need multiple visits
Custom Quote
